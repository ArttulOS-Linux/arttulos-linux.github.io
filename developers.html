Natalie Spiva

Co-Lead of AcreetionOS

Natalie is a specialist in high-performance Linux architecture and security-first design. As the Co-Lead of AcreetionOS, she has spent years immersive in the Arch Linux ecosystem, building tools that prioritize user freedom and technical transparency.

ArttulOS represents her vision of a "Sovereign Hybrid"—a system where the user no longer has to choose between the immutable stability of enterprise environments and the bleeding-edge availability of rolling releases. It is a solo project, built from the ground up to explore the "greys" of computing.

When she's not debugging kernel shims or optimizing package orchestration, Natalie is usually consuming excessive amounts of coffee or analyzing the intersection of psychological science and software engineering.
