Ermin_Evolution.

Redefining collaboration infrastructure. Ermin is the dedicated task-tracking and project management suite for the ArttulOS ecosystem.

Beyond Legacy Tools

In our pursuit of technical integrity, we have moved beyond legacy platforms that failed to meet our standards for self-hosting and privacy. Ermin is a fundamental rebuild, designed to ensure that the infrastructure supporting ArttulOS is as sovereign as the OS itself.

"A self-hosted solution should empower the user, not burden them with convoluted architecture."

Decentralized_Focus

Ermin is built as a free and open-source alternative to centralized project management silos. It reflects our core values: performance, transparency, and user freedom.

Development_Status

Currently in active development. We are focusing on a clean, efficient codebase that can scale alongside the ArttulOS community and its unique hybrid requirements.
